# ShoCode Venue Research / 会場リサーチ

> Compiled by Naruto (Operations) for Drew Robbins
> Status: **UPDATED 2026-04-02 — Another Day closing; new top-3 identified**

## Requirements / 要件

- Capacity: 50–100 people / 収容人数: 50〜100名
- Reliable WiFi for 50–100 simultaneous developer connections / 開発者50〜100名の同時接続に耐えるWiFi
- Adequate power outlets for laptops at every table / 全テーブルにPC用電源
- Presentation space (projector/screen, mic) / プレゼンスペース（プロジェクター・スクリーン・マイク）
- Food/catering options / 食事・ケータリング対応
- Accessible from Tokyo (ideally < 90 min by train) / 東京からアクセス良好（電車90分以内が理想）
- Shonan coastal atmosphere / 湘南の雰囲気
- Budget: affordable for a community event / 予算: コミュニティイベントとして手頃

- **Power:** Full conference infrastructure
- **AV Equipment:** Projector, screen, whiteboard included in seminar packages. Simultaneous interpretation booths available.
- **Cost:**
  - Seminar packages from ¥17,000–19,000/person (overnight, 3 meals, conference room)

  - **Note:** Price revision effective April 1, 2026
- **Vibe:** Professional retreat center, not beachy. Beautiful mountain/nature setting but lacks the coastal Shonan vibe. Used by corporates (TOTO, NII, etc.). Feels more like an offsite than a community event.
- **Pros:**
  - Most professional and reliable venue — everything works
  - Best WiFi guarantee (used for international academic conferences)

  - Isolated hilltop location — no "Shonan coastal" feel

  - Corporate retreat atmosphere may not fit ShoCode's community vibe
