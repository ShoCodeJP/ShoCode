# ShoCode Venue Research / 会場リサーチ

> Compiled by Naruto (Operations) for Drew Robbins
> Status: **Research delivered — awaiting site visits and final decision**

## Requirements / 要件

- Capacity: 50–100 people / 収容人数: 50〜100名
- Reliable WiFi for 50–100 simultaneous developer connections / 開発者50〜100名の同時接続に耐えるWiFi
- Adequate power outlets for laptops at every table / 全テーブルにPC用電源
- Presentation space (projector/screen, mic) / プレゼンスペース（プロジェクター・スクリーン・マイク）
- Food/catering options / 食事・ケータリング対応
- Accessible from Tokyo (ideally < 90 min by train) / 東京からアクセス良好（電車90分以内が理想）
- Shonan coastal atmosphere / 湘南の雰囲気
- Budget: affordable for a community event / 予算: コミュニティイベントとして手頃

### 2. Hayama Marina Rental Space (Emerald Room)

- **Website:** <https://hayamamarina.com/rentalspace/> — ✅ Live and accessible. Full rental info, pricing, and room details available.
- **Contact:** TEL 046-875-0002 / FAX 046-876-1146 (no public email found; phone inquiry recommended)
- **Location:** Hayama Marina, 〒240-0112 神奈川県三浦郡葉山町堀内50-2
- **Access from Tokyo:** ~90 min (JR to Zushi/逗子, then bus to 葉山マリーナ stop). Kyukei Line to 逗子・葉山駅 also an option.
- **Capacity:** Emerald Room — 117㎡, max 100 people. Seated party: 60. Standing: 100. Conference/lecture: 60.
- **Food:** Catering consultation available on-site (ケータリングの相談も承ります)
- **WiFi:** Not explicitly mentioned — **must verify**. Marina venues can be spotty.
- **Power:** Not detailed — needs confirmation for 50+ laptops
- **AV Equipment (rental, tax included):**
  - Podium: ¥1,100
  - Sound system (2 mics): ¥3,300
  - Whiteboard: ¥1,100
  - Projector + screen: ¥5,500
- **Cost:**
  - Daytime (9:00–17:00): ¥6,600/hour (tax included)
  - Evening (17:00–21:00): ¥9,900/hour (tax included)
  - Full day estimate (9:00–19:00): ~¥6,600 × 8h + ¥9,900 × 2h = **¥72,600** + equipment
- **Vibe:** Stunning — panoramic views of Sagami Bay and Mt. Fuji from the Emerald Room (相模湾から富士山まで一望できる大空間). Premium marina setting. Feels special.
- **Pros:**
  - Best views of any venue on this list — ocean + Fuji
  - Capacity fits our needs perfectly at 100
  - AV equipment available on-site
  - Reasonable hourly rate for the setting
  - Catering support available
- **Cons:**
  - Access requires bus from Zushi station — adds 15–20 min and complexity
  - WiFi is a major unknown and a critical risk
  - Power outlet situation unclear for a developer event
  - Evening rates jump significantly
- **Action items:** Inquire about WiFi (can they provide or allow us to bring portable APs?), power outlet availability, and catering menu/pricing

### 3. Shonan Village Center / 湘南国際村センター (Hayama)

- **Website:** <https://www.shonan-village.co.jp/> — ✅ Live and accessible. Contact form: <https://www.shonan-village.co.jp/mailform_jp>. TEL: 046-855-1800.
- **Location:** Hayama hilltop (上山口), surrounded by nature. 〒240-0198 神奈川県三浦郡葉山町上山口1560-39
- **Access from Tokyo:** ~90 min. Bus from Zushi required. The center can arrange bus transport.
- **Capacity:** 24 conference rooms of various sizes. Up to 200+ with multiple rooms. 100 guest rooms for overnight stays.
- **Food:** On-site restaurant. Supports special dietary needs (vegetarian, etc.). Party/banquet catering available.
- **WiFi:** Conference-grade — used by NII (National Institute of Informatics) for international academic conferences. This is the most reliable WiFi option.
- **Power:** Full conference infrastructure
- **AV Equipment:** Projector, screen, whiteboard included in seminar packages. Simultaneous interpretation booths available.
- **Cost:**
  - Seminar packages from ¥17,000–19,000/person (overnight, 3 meals, conference room)
  - Day-use pricing available but not listed — requires inquiry
  - **Note:** Price revision effective April 1, 2026
- **Vibe:** Professional retreat center, not beachy. Beautiful mountain/nature setting but lacks the coastal Shonan vibe. Used by corporates (TOTO, NII, etc.). Feels more like an offsite than a community event.
- **Pros:**
  - Most professional and reliable venue — everything works
  - Best WiFi guarantee (used for international academic conferences)
  - 24-hour room occupancy possible for intensive events
  - Dedicated coordinator support from planning through execution
  - English-language support available
  - Can handle overnight if we ever expand to multi-day
- **Cons:**
  - Isolated hilltop location — no "Shonan coastal" feel
  - Bus-dependent access
  - Overnight packages are expensive for a community event (~¥17K–19K/person)
  - Day-use pricing unclear
  - Corporate retreat atmosphere may not fit ShoCode's community vibe
- **Action items:** Inquire about day-use only pricing. Could work as a backup/rainy-day option.

### 4. Fujisawa Civic Hall / 藤沢市民会館 Area Facilities

- **Website:** Fujisawa city facilities — <https://www.city.fujisawa.kanagawa.jp/>
- **Location:** Central Fujisawa, near Fujisawa station
- **Access from Tokyo:** ~50 min via JR Tokaido Line — **best train access of all options**
- **Capacity:** Municipal halls range from 50–500 depending on room
- **Food:** No on-site catering — external catering or nearby restaurants (Fujisawa station area has many options)
- **WiFi:** Municipal — likely basic. Would need to bring portable WiFi.
- **Power:** Available but may need supplementation with power strips
- **AV Equipment:** Typically available at municipal venues
- **Cost:** Municipal rates — very affordable (typically ¥5,000–20,000 for a full day depending on room)
- **Vibe:** Standard municipal facility. Functional but not inspiring. No ocean atmosphere.
- **Pros:**
  - Best train access from Tokyo (Tokaido Line direct)
  - Very affordable
  - Proven event infrastructure
  - Close to restaurants and convenience stores
- **Cons:**
  - Zero Shonan atmosphere — could be anywhere in Japan
  - WiFi likely insufficient for developers
  - No ocean views or outdoor space
  - Booking municipal facilities can involve bureaucracy
- **Action items:** If budget is the primary concern, investigate specific rooms and availability. Not recommended as primary choice due to lack of atmosphere.

### 7. Beach-adjacent Venue Option: Enoshima Area

- **Location:** Enoshima / Katase-Enoshima station area
- **Access from Tokyo:** ~70 min (Odakyu Romancecar to Katase-Enoshima, or JR + Enoden)
- **Concept:** Several restaurants and event spaces along the Enoshima coast. The area has strong tourist infrastructure and a quintessential Shonan feel.
- **Potential venues to scout:**
  - Restaurant/event spaces along Katase Higashihama beach
  - Enoshima Island Spa conference facilities
  - Hotels near Enoshima with event rooms (e.g., Enoshima area ryokan with banquet halls)
- **Vibe:** Peak Shonan. Beach, island views, surfer culture. High "wow factor" for out-of-town attendees.
- **Risks:** Tourist-oriented venues may lack developer infrastructure (WiFi, power). Summer season = peak pricing and crowds.
- **Action items:** Scout along the coast during a weekday to identify specific venues. A beachside restaurant that can accommodate 60–80 would be a dream venue.

### 8. Chigasaki Community / Cultural Center Area

- **Location:** Near Chigasaki station
- **Access from Tokyo:** ~55 min via JR Tokaido Line
- **Capacity:** Community centers typically handle 50–150
- **Food:** External catering; Chigasaki has a good local restaurant scene
- **WiFi/Power:** Basic municipal infrastructure
- **Cost:** Very affordable (municipal pricing)
- **Vibe:** Chigasaki has a laid-back surf-town atmosphere, but municipal facilities don't capture that. The town itself is charming — could pair a municipal venue with an after-party at a local beach restaurant.
- **Pros:** Affordable, decent capacity, good train access
- **Cons:** Municipal facility = generic atmosphere. Similar downsides to Fujisawa option.
- **Action items:** Only consider if other venues are unavailable or significantly over budget.
