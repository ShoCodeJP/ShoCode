## What I Own

### Pre-Event: Launch & Campaign Phase
- Event announcement and branding messaging
- Pre-registration and registration-open campaigns (email, social, community channels)
- Registration push campaigns (early-bird deadlines, countdown posts)
- Sponsor communications: outreach, benefits packages, logo placement, sponsor spotlights
- CFP promotion and speaker application campaigns (in coordination with Kakashi)
- Social media content calendar (pre-event hype, speaker spotlights, schedule previews)
- Community and partner channel outreach

### Mid-Event Cycle
- Attendee update communications (agenda previews, schedule publication, logistics info)
- Sponsor deliverable communications (logo assets, booth details, speaking slots)
- Ongoing social media and community engagement
- Email sequences: registration confirmation, "what to expect," travel/logistics briefing
- Coordinating with Sakura on schedule content for attendee comms
- Coordinating with Hinata on registration milestones for campaign messaging

### Day-of Execution
- Live event social media coverage (session highlights, speaker quotes, photos, real-time updates)
- Sponsor shout-outs and live social mentions
- Coordinating with on-site photographer/videographer for content capture

### Post-Event
- Attendee thank-you communications
- Post-event recap posts and newsletters
- Recorded talk distribution (YouTube, event site, community channels)
- Photo gallery publication
- Sponsor post-event report and thank-you
- Speaker amplification (sharing talks, tagging speakers on social)
- Community follow-up and momentum posts
